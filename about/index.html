About Me

Hello, world!

I'm Arek, or as I'm known on the 'net, arkus - a seasoned software engineer with a passion for all things code.

My journey

I've journeyed from mobile development (both Android and iOS) to backend development with Node.js. I found that the challenges and intricacies of backend development suited my problem-solving nature more than mobile applications. Although it's much harder to explain to my parents what I do for a living now, at least I'm not being asked to change the color of a button every other day by clients :)

Education and hobbies

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science, which laid the foundation for my career. But the real learning happened on the job, where I faced and overcame numerous challenges. I believe in sharing knowledge, and that's what I aim to do here on this website.

When I'm not lost in the world of code, you can find me immersed in games, ranging from multiplayer FPS like CS:GO or CoD41 to narrative-driven games like Detroit: Become Human. I also occasionally swing a tennis racket and dream of embarking on a long motorcycle trip someday.

What to expect from this blog?

The main theme of this blog is programming concepts, things I've recently learned, and insights I want to share.

I'm particularly fascinated by the Rust programming language, and I plan to delve deeper into it (and perhaps other low-level languages? Time will tell).

From time to time, I'll also share some of the problems I've encountered in my work (mostly with TypeScript/Node.js) and how I've solved them.

And who knows, I might occasionally drop in something personal, like tales from a trip I've taken.

Clean code, dirty mind

I believe in maintaining the highest standards in my work while keeping a playful and open mind.

Welcome to my blog, where we'll explore the world of programming together.
